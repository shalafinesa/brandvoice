Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026 · Effective immediately

🔒 Short version: We collect only what we need to run this service. We don't sell your data. We don't share it with advertisers. You can delete your account and all your data at any time.

1. Who we are

Brand Voice is a product created by Finesa Shala ("we", "us", "our"). We provide an AI-powered social media content generation service accessible at finesashala-brandvoice.netlify.app.

For any privacy-related questions, contact us at: privacy@brandvoice.app

2. What data we collect

We collect the minimum data necessary to provide our service:

  • Account data: Your email address and encrypted password when you create an account.
  • Brand profile data: The business information you enter (name, niche, product description, target audience, tone of voice, keywords). This is stored locally in your browser and used only to generate your content.
  • Usage data: Anonymous analytics about how you use the app (page views, button clicks, time on site) via Google Analytics.
  • Technical data: IP address, browser type, device type — collected automatically by our hosting provider (Netlify) for security and performance purposes.

We do not collect: payment card details (handled by Stripe directly), the content you generate, or any data about your social media accounts.

3. How we use your data

  • To create and manage your account
  • To generate social media content based on your brand profile
  • To send you transactional emails (account confirmation, password reset)
  • To understand how people use our product and improve it
  • To protect against abuse and unauthorized use of our service
  • To comply with legal obligations

4. Legal basis for processing (GDPR)

Under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), we process your personal data on the following legal bases:

  • Contract performance: Processing your account data and brand profile to deliver the service you signed up for.
  • Legitimate interests: Security monitoring, fraud prevention, and service improvement.
  • Consent: Analytics cookies — you can withdraw consent at any time via the cookie banner.

5. Cookies

We use two types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: Required for authentication and keeping you logged in. These cannot be disabled.
  • Analytics cookies: Google Analytics (GA4) to understand site traffic and usage patterns. These are only set with your consent.

You can manage your cookie preferences at any time using the cookie settings button at the bottom of the page.

6. Third-party services

We use the following third-party services to operate Brand Voice:

  • Netlify (netlify.com) — hosting, serverless functions, authentication. Data processed in the USA under Standard Contractual Clauses.
  • Anthropic (anthropic.com) — AI content generation. Your prompts are sent to Anthropic's API to generate content. Anthropic does not use your prompts to train their models by default.
  • Google Analytics (analytics.google.com) — website analytics. Data processed in the USA. Only activated with your consent.
  • Stripe (stripe.com) — payment processing (coming soon). Stripe handles all payment data directly — we never see your card details.

7. Data retention

  • Account data: Kept for as long as your account is active. Deleted within 30 days of account deletion.
  • Brand profile: Stored in your browser's localStorage — deleted when you clear your browser data or delete your account.
  • Analytics data: Retained for 14 months by Google Analytics (their default).
  • Server logs: Retained for 30 days by Netlify for security purposes.

8. Your rights under GDPR

If you are located in the European Union or EEA, you have the following rights:

  • ✅ Right of access: Request a copy of your personal data.
  • ✅ Right to rectification: Correct inaccurate data we hold about you.
  • ✅ Right to erasure: Request deletion of your personal data ("right to be forgotten").
  • ✅ Right to restriction: Request we limit how we process your data.
  • ✅ Right to portability: Receive your data in a machine-readable format.
  • ✅ Right to object: Object to processing based on legitimate interests.
  • ✅ Right to withdraw consent: Withdraw analytics consent at any time.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@brandvoice.app. We will respond within 30 days.

You also have the right to lodge a complaint with your national data protection authority. In Germany, this is the Bundesbeauftragter für den Datenschutz (BfDI).

9. Data security

We take security seriously. Our measures include:

  • All data transmitted over HTTPS (TLS encryption)
  • Authentication handled by Netlify Identity with JWT tokens
  • API keys stored as encrypted environment variables — never in code
  • Rate limiting on all API endpoints to prevent abuse
  • No storage of payment card data (handled entirely by Stripe)

10. Children's privacy

Brand Voice is not directed at children under 16. We do not knowingly collect personal data from anyone under 16. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us and we will delete it immediately.

11. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page and notify registered users by email for significant changes.
