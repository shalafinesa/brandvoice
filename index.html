Platforms

Every platform has its own rules.

A LinkedIn post that works is nothing like an Instagram caption. We write each one the way that platform rewards engagement.

💼
LinkedIn
Hook + 3–4 insight paragraphs + CTA. 5–7 niche hashtags. Drives profile views, connections and inbound leads.
📸
Instagram
Punchy caption + 15–20 hashtags (broad, niche, micro). Ends with a question that triggers comments and saves.
🐦
X / Twitter
Under 280 characters. Sharp and opinionated. Built to be shared and drive new followers.
👥
Facebook
Community-first tone, 120–200 words. Opens with a relatable question. Designed to drive comments and shares.
🎵
TikTok Script
3-second hook + 30–45 second body + 5-second CTA. Three-part structure. Ready to film instantly.
